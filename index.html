GRAVITY STICKMAN

PRIVACY POLICY  ·  Last updated: March 2026

📋 Overview

Gravity Stickman is a mobile game developed by Abdulatif Saidrasulov. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

✅  This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

📱 Device Sensors

Gravity Stickman uses your device's accelerometer sensor to detect tilt and shake gestures. This is the core mechanic of the game — tilting controls gravity, shaking launches the stickman.

Sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

✓ Never stored ✓ Never transmitted ✓ Local only

💾 Local Storage

The app saves your high score locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is only used to display your personal best score in the game.

High score (number only) ✓ Stored on device only

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

✗ Name or identity ✗ Email address ✗ Location data ✗ Device identifiers ✗ Usage analytics ✗ Crash reports ✗ Camera or microphone ✗ Contacts or files

📢 Advertising

Gravity Stickman contains no advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs or tracking technologies.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 or any user of any age, as described above. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, changes are unlikely to affect users in any meaningful way.
